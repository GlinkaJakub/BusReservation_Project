Dashboard

It's an application for managing a bus agensy and ticket booking system.
It was creates as the final project in the subject 'Wykorzystanie MS SQL i pakietów MS Business Intelligence do budowy aplikacji' at the Warsaw University of Technology.
The application is being developed and may facilite the management of company in the future.

  I wrote backend it in Java and Spring Boot, but to wrote frontend I used Bootstrap Studio for students.

For now if you are admin click one of option: (Agency, Buses, Trips, Profile). But if you are a client choose one of options in menu (Trips or Profile). 
I tried hidden admins options for users, but thymeleaf doesn't want cooperate :/
